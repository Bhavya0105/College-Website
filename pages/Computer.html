Department of Computer Science and Engineering.

Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in 1997 and since then is the most sought after Department of the Institute. The BTech programme is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) under Tier-I category. The Department has always kept pace with the rapid advancements in the field.

The Department is known for its strength in Theoretical Computer Science, Image Processing and Computer Vision, High Performance Computing, Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning, Internet of Things, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Networking and Cyber Security. The combination of qualified and committed faculty, a strong peer group, core and inter-disciplinary research, collaboration with leading industries and research organisations offers students of this department an enriching learning experience.
